Latitude vs. Cloudiness

The correlation between Latitude and Cloudiness also appears to be very low as noted by the shape of the scatter, a generally flat relationship between the two variables, and a high amount of variance at each degree of Latitude.

There are some interesting clusters of Cloudiness percentage measurements at 0%, 20%, ~75% and ~95%. Further analysis is required but my initial explanation would attribute this to common weather patterns (i.e. weather moves in fronts and often presents as clear and sunny (~0% cloudiness) or overcast and rain (~100% cloudiness).
