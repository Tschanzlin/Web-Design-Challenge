Latitude vs. Tempurature

The data exhibits a strong correlation between latitude and tempurature as you would expect, and as confirmed by a strong r value. The data also exhibits a non-linear relationship beteween Latitude and Tempurature, with more pronounced changes in tempurate at outside of +/- 20 degrees Latitude (approximate slope of +/-1 vs a slope closer to 0 within +/- 20 degrees Latitude.

While the data appears to be more strongly correlated with the Northern Hemisphere (positive degress of latitude), this is due to more northern hemisphere cities from the citypy data pull. According to Wikipedia, the Northern Hemisphere has most of the earth's landmass and 90% of the population.
