Latitude vs. Humidity

The data exhibits very little to no correlation between latitude and humidity as demonstrated by the shape of the scatter plot. A regresssion line of the data has a slope of roughly zero, denoting little change in the y-value (Latitude) as the x-value changes (Humidity). The data is also has a high degree of variability at each degree of latitude.
