Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The data exhibits very little correlation between Latitude and Wind Speed as noted by shape of scatter -- relatively flat trend line and high amount of Wind Speed variation at each degree of Latitude.

While high, the variation in Wind Speed does appear to be roughly equally distributed across all degress of latitude, with the exception of slightly higher Wind Speeds at extreme nothern locations. With more datpoints in the Southern Hemisphere, it would be reasonable to assume that this would also be seen in the Southern Hemisphere as well.
